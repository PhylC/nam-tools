Strategic objectives

Three objectives guide every commercial decision.

1. Grow quality distribution

  • Close gaps on high-ROS SKUs.
  • Protect core shelf presence.

2. Improve investment return

  • Reduce low-ROI deep cuts.
  • Move spend into feature and launch windows.

3. Build premium mix

  • Grow sharing and baked ranges.
  • Add £480k gross profit.
